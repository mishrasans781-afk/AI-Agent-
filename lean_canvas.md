# Lean Canvas - Study Guidance Bot

| Topic | Content |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Problem** | 1. **Lack of Personalization**: Students struggle to find study schedules that fit their specific learning pace and availability.<br>2. **Information Overload**: Learners are overwhelmed by the vast amount of uncurated content available online.<br>3. **Limited Access to Tutors**: Quality private tutoring is often expensive and not available 24/7 for instant doubts.<br>4. **Procrastination**: Students often lack the motivation or structure to start studying without external guidance.<br>5. **Inefficient Revision**: Difficulty in identifying weak areas and structuring a revision plan effectively before exams. |
| **Solution** | 1. **AI-Powered Study Plans**: Generates custom schedules and roadmaps based on the user's syllabus and timeframe.<br>2. **Instant Query Resolution**: A 24/7 chatbot (StudyBot) that answers academic questions instantly using the Gemini model.<br>3. **Resource Curation**: Suggests specific topics and breakdowns rather than generic advice.<br>4. **Interactive Learning**: Engaging chat interface that makes studying feel like a conversation rather than a chore.<br>5. **Smart Adaptation**: The bot can explain concepts in different ways (simply, detailed, etc.) based on user feedback. |
| **Unique Value Proposition** | 1. **"Your Personal AI Tutor"**: A distinct promise of having a smart companion rather than just a search engine.<br>2. **Powered by Gemini 2.0**: Leverages one of the latest and fastest AI models for superior reasoning and speed.<br>3. **Zero-Friction UI**: A clean, distraction-free interface designed specifically for studying, unlike cluttered educational sites.<br>4. **Context-Aware**: Unlike generic ChatGPT, this is fine-tuned (via system prompts) specifically for educational guidance.<br>5. **Always Available**: Democratizes access to high-quality educational support regardless of time or location. |
| **Unfair Advantage** | 1. **Specialized Prompt Engineering**: Custom system instructions in `graph.py` that optimize outputs for education.<br>2. **Modern Tech Stack**: Fast and scalable architecture using FastAPI and React with Vite.<br>3. **Integration of LangGraph**: Capability to handle complex, multi-step reasoning workflows (future-proofing for complex tasks).<br>4. **Cost-Effective Scalability**: built on efficient models allowing for a robust free tier to capture market share.<br>5. **Rapid Deployment**: The ability to quickly iterate and deploy updates (as seen with Render/Vercel usage) faster than traditional EdTech giants. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **High School Students**: Preparing for board exams or competitive entrance tests.<br>2. **University Students**: Needing help with complex assignments or thesis research.<br>3. **Self-Learners**: Individuals upskilling in programming, data science, or new languages.<br>4. **Educators**: Looking for tools to help generate lesson ideas or explanations for students.<br>5. **Parents**: Seeking affordable and reliable supplemental education tools for their children. |
| **Key Metrics** | 1. **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Number of students using the bot daily.<br>2. **Session Duration**: How long a user engages with the bot in a single study session.<br>3. **Retention Rate**: The percentage of users who return after their first week.<br>4. **Query Success Rate**: How often users accept the AI's answer without needing to rephrase (indicated by positive feedback).<br>5. **Plan Completion**: Percentage of generated study plans that users actually follow through or interact with. |
| **Channels** | 1. **Student Communities**: Discord servers, Reddit (r/homeworkhelp, etc.), and student forums.<br>2. **Social Media**: TikTok and Instagram Reels demonstrating "homework hacks" with the bot.<br>3. **SEO / Content Marketing**: Blogs about "how to study effectively" driving traffic to the app.<br>4. **Direct Referrals**: "Share with a classmate" features within the app.<br>5. **Educational Partners**: Collaborations with schools or tutoring centers to use the tool as an aid. |
| **Cost Structure** | 1. **LLM Infrastructure**: Costs associated with API calls to Google Gemini (token usage).<br>2. **Hosting & Cloud**: Server costs for the backend (Render) and frontend (Vercel/Netlify).<br>3. **Development**: Time and effort spent on coding, maintenance, and bug fixing.<br>4. **Domain & DNS**: Annual fees for the custom web domain.<br>5. **Marketing**: Budget for social media ads or influencer partnerships. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Freemium Model**: Basic Q&A is free; advanced features (long-term planning, file analysis) are paid.<br>2. **Subscription**: Monthly fee for "Pro" access with faster models and unlimited history.<br>3. **Affiliate Marketing**: Commissions from recommending specific textbooks or paid courses.<br>4. **Tutor Marketplace**: Connecting students with human tutors if the AI cannot solve a complex problem (commission-based).<br>5. **B2B Licensing**: Selling the customized version of the bot to schools or universities. |
